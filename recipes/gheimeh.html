Gheimeh recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef or lamb, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 dried Persian limes (limu omani), pierced or broken in pieces
  • 4 cups water or broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Potato strips for topping

Steps

  1. Saute chopped onions in oil until translucent.
  2. Add cubed meat and brown lightly.
  3. Stir in turmeric, salt, pepper, tomato paste.
  4. Add split peas and dried limes; cover with water or broth.
  5. Simmer gently for 1â€“2 hours until meat and peas are tender.
  6. Fry potato strips until golden, drain, and lightly salt.
  7. Serve the stew over rice and top with crispy potatoes.
