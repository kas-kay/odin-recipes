Ghormeh sabzi recipe

Description

Ghormeh sabzi, also known as Khoresht sabzi, also spelled qormeh sabzi, is an Iranian herb stew.

It is considered the national dish and is a very popular dish in Iran.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef or lamb, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup red kidney beans, soaked or canned
  • 4 dried Persian limes (limu omani), pierced
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 bunch parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
  • 4-5 green onions or scallions, chopped (green parts)
  • 2 tbsp dried fenugreek leaves
  • 2 cups spinach or chard, chopped
  • Salt, pepper, oil

Steps

  1. Sauté chopped onions in oil until golden.
  2. Add cubed meat, turmeric, salt, and pepper; brown the meat.
  3. Mix in kidney beans, cover with water, and simmer.
  4. Sauté chopped herbs and fenugreek in a separate pan until wilted and fragrant.
  5. Add fried herbs and dried limes to the stew; simmer for 1–2 hours until the meat is tender and the flavors meld.
  6. Adjust seasoning and add a little lemon juice for extra tang if desired.
  7. Serve hot with white rice.
