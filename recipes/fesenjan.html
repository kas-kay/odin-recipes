Fesenjan recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken (thighs or leg quarters, bone-in, skin removed)
  • 3–4 cups walnuts, finely ground
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 cup pomegranate molasses (plus more as needed)
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2–3 tbsp oil
  • Water (enough to cover)

Steps

  1. Dry-roast the walnuts in a pan or oven until golden, then grind them finely.
  2. Sauté the onion in oil until golden. Add chicken pieces and brown on all sides
  3. Add turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper; stir well.
  4. Add ground walnuts and enough water to cover. Bring to a low simmer and cook for about 30 minutes.
  5. Stir in pomegranate molasses and sugar/honey. Simmer gently (lid on) for 1–2 hours until the sauce is thickened, rich, and chicken is tender. Adjust sugar and pomegranate molasses for desired sweetness/sourness.
  6. Garnish with pomegranate seeds if available. Serve with steamed rice.
